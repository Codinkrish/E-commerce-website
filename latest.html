PAY LATER

Redstore Pay Later is the hassle-free way to get instant credit, via a completely digital process, for purchases using EMI on Redstore.in. You have to complete the one- time setup process, which should not take more than 2 minutes and does not require you to provide credit card details. Once the setup is complete, you can avail Redstore Pay Later payment option during checkout on Redstore.in, and pay later next month or over EMIs ranging from 3 to 12 months. You can easily track your purchases, repayments, and limits history from a simplified dashboard for this payment mode. Redstore Pay Later is offered to you by Redstore Pay (India) Private Limited ("Redstore") in partnership with one of its third-party lending partners - axio or IDFC FIRST Bank.

You need to have an Redstore.in account with a verified mobile number, a valid permanent account number (PAN) card, bank account with one of the selected banks, and one of the officially valid document as address proof - Driving license, Voter ID card, Aadhaar, Utility Bills (not older than 60 days), Passport. You must be 23 years of age or above Further, eligibility is decided basis information already available with Amazon and your credit bureau history. Based on these and a host of other factors, lending partner will decide the Redstore Pay Later limit for you.
